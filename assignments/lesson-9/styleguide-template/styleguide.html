Overview

Site Purpose

This website is my portfolio. It's purpose is to show my work to clients and future employers. I will be displaying my paintings, my graphic designs, and my photography.

Target Audience

My target audience is any potemtial client who might want to buy my paintings, designs, or photography. I will also target future employers who might want to see my protfolio. I need to make my site professional while also setting myself and my work apart from others.

Color Palette

Artwork looks best on white background. Like you see in galleries, I want to use mostly white so there is no distraction from the artwork. But I do need some color to keep consistency with my brand. These colors will be used sparingly and tie into the logo nicely. I chose these colors because they capture the look I'm going for and stand out well next to eachother.

Typography

I chose a serif font named Stempel Garamond LT Std because I feel like it's professional and matches my logo and title. I chose a contrasting font for the body which is a sans-serif named Kohinoor Devanagari. I love pairing a sans-serif and a serif together because it adds the contrast that most websites need.
